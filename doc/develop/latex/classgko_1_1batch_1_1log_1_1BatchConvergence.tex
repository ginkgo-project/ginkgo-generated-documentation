\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename Value\+Type = default\+\_\+precision$>$\newline
class gko\+::batch\+::log\+::\+Batch\+Convergence$<$ Value\+Type $>$}

Logs the final residuals and iteration counts for a batch solver. 

The purpose of this logger is to give simple access to standard data generated by the solver once it has converged.

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
The final logged residuals are the implicit residuals that have been computed within the solver process. Depending on the solver algorithm, this may be significantly different from the true residual ($\vert$$\vert$b -\/ Ax$\vert$$\vert$). 
\end{DoxyNote}

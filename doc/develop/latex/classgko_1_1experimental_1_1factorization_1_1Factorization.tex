\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename Value\+Type, typename Index\+Type$>$\newline
class gko\+::experimental\+::factorization\+::\+Factorization$<$ Value\+Type, Index\+Type $>$}

Represents a generic factorization consisting of two triangular factors (upper and lower) and an optional diagonal scaling matrix. 

This class is used to represent a wide range of different factorizations to be passed on to direct solvers and other similar operations. The storage\+\_\+type represents how the individual factors are stored internally\+: They may be stored as separate matrices or in a single matrix, and be symmetric or unsymmetric, with the diagonal belonging to both factory, a single factor or being a separate scaling factor (Cholesky vs. L\+D\+L$^\wedge$H vs. LU vs. L\+DU).

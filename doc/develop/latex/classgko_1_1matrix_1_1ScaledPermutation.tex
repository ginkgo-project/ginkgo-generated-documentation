\mbox{\hyperlink{classgko_1_1matrix_1_1ScaledPermutation}{Scaled\+Permutation}} is a matrix combining a permutation with scaling factors. 

It is a combination of \mbox{\hyperlink{classgko_1_1matrix_1_1Diagonal}{Diagonal}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classgko_1_1matrix_1_1Permutation}{Permutation}}, and can be read as $SP = P \cdot S$, i.\+e. the scaling gets applied before the permutation.
